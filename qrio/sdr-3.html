SDR-3

In 1997, Sony started the Sony Dreams Robot (SDR) project which aimed to build a small bipedal humnoid robot that would be used for the future of home entertainment. Two key elements were at the forefront of this concept: "motion entertainment" and "communication entertainment".

The SDR timeline starts with a few robots that we know nothing about; SDR-0, SDR-1, and SDR-2. Next is SDR-3 and still, nothing is known about it, however we do have two pictures.

Some observations, it looks like th head can either be actuated down or folded down into the recessed chest area.
