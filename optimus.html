OPTIMUS

Pre Development

On August 19th, 2021, following many self driving and Dojo super computer announcments, it was unveiled that Tesla was in the hiring stage to build a humanoid robot. They planned to mass produce them using their knowledge in car manufacturing, making it cost effective for anyone to buy.

At a height of 5'8", weighing 125 lbs and containing 40 actuators, it is designed to carry out simple tasks without explicit instruction. There was also an emphasis on how the robot would be slow enought to out run and weak enough to over power, just incase. It would use the same FSD (full self-driving) computer found in their production cars.
